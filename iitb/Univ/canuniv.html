Canadian Universities

Web home pages

This list includes all institutions that are members of the Association of Universities and Colleges of Canada, as well as a few others that may be of special importance. If the name of the university followed by an asterisk*, the reference is to a gopher main menu, for lack of a Web home page. The names of institutions with no known home page or main menu are also listed.

This page is maintained by Chris Redmond at the University of Waterloo, credmond@uwaterloo.ca. Corrections and updates are very welcome. (Click here to mail.)
