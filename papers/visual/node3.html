Visual Cue based Formant Tracking

In this section we propose a scheme to identify and track formants in a speech signal using the visual cues present in its spectrogram. The proposed scheme is based on inspection of the spectrogram to identify the spectral peaks or formants using simple image processing techniques and later track the formants over time.
