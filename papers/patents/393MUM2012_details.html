About the Patent

Title, Country, Grant Date

  • A System for Conversational Quality Monitoring of Call center Conversation and a Method Thereof.
  • USA (Granted: 24-AUG-2016)
  • CHINA, INDIA (Specifications)

What domain area does the patent address?

  • Improving a conversation quality of calls in a call center setup/environment.
  • monitoring of several live ongoing audio conversations
  • alert supervisor regarding vulnerable/deviations in the call being handled by a call center agent

Are you working on any additional patent filings for enhancements?

Several (related)

  • Emotion extraction in speech (in almost real time)
  • Robustness in speech to text conversion

In which domain the patent can be used?

Applicable to Call centers, specifically voice based call centers.

Who are the customers or target customers

Any enterprise that runs a call center to address queries of their customers is a potential user of this patent. Assuming that most enterprises desire a high customer satisfaction score when they try to resolve the queries of their customers.

Is there productivity savings because of the patent?

Yes. Considering the fact that an unhappy customer is a lost source of income to an enterprise. This patent helps in monitoring the conversational quality in real time (as the call is in progress) and enables a supervisor to intervene and convert a possible "bad for the enterprise" call into a retained customer.
