Features Used

Feature selection is an important aspect of image interpretation. The features used in all our experimental work are (Table G.2, Appendix G): (i) Single node cliques: area , perimeter , average gray level , the mass center , variance , compactness and (ii) Two node cliques: contrast , common perimeter ratio or boundary length .
