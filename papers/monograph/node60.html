Experimental Results

Experiments were carried out to validate the proposed scheme of joint segmentation and image interpretation in the framework of modular integration and multiresolution. Tests were conducted both on real outdoor (road images) and indoor (computer images) images of size which were either captured using the QuickTake100 digital camera (Figures in Section 4.2) or using an aim and shoot Kodak Pro 111 camera and then scanning using the HP Color Scanner (Figures in Section 4.3).
